My Agenda

Plan
>
Let's go through the steps of the proposed plan as General Secretary:
Step-1: Make an Agenda that positively affects maximum number of students.
Step-2: Check for Viability of the plans.
Step-3: Actually implement the accepted plans in a planned and proper method.
1. Scheduled buses from the main gate to Panjim and Madgaon on weekends
>
Problem: Travel is a sore spot in the life of every BITSian.
Proposal:
  • Regulate a Weekend Travel System.
  • Two buses (to start with) take students to Panaji and Madgaon.
Viability:
  • Have a minimum number of seats booked for buses to run.
  • Issue either a weekly pass or a one-time payment system to have an estimation of bookings.
2. An air-conditioned study area that is Open 24x7
>
Problem:
  • No Comfortable Areas to work/study are open 24x7.
  • The Weather makes studying in the rooms very tough at times.
Proposal:
  • Keep a designated area open 24 hrs.
  • This could be either a section of the library, a DLT, or even a couple of classrooms.
Viability: Less personnel required than keeping the whole library open.
3. Bringing vending machines to campus
>
Problem:
  • No 24x7 Eateries on campus for sudden hunger!
  • This becomes essential especially during exams.
Proposal:
  • Install Vending machines around popular spots on campus.
  • Make sure they're regularly restocked and maintained over time.
Viability:
  • Easy to arrange and stock with basic snacks and drinks.
  • No manpower required in general to maintain.
4. Option to use money from our SWD accounts for shops inside campus
>
Problem: The money in the SWD account is not being utilized very efficiently.
Proposal:
  • Research a seamless way to integrate payments with the student's account.
  • Try out either a QR system or pay through ID cards.
Problem: We'll have a one-stop portal (CSA app), which has all the information we would ever need on a daily basis
5. Improve Internet Connectivity and Unban certain games on campus-net
>
Problem:
  • The range of campus wifi does not cover important areas like LTs and Messes.
  • All video games and related software have been banned
Proposal:
  • Install more routers across campus to have a stable range across the institute.
  • Unban a certain number of the most popular games every semester by student choice.
Viability:
  • A better connection would in general boost productivity.
  • Still maintain the 8GB daily data limit to prevent us from going overboard with gaming.
